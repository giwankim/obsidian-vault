> [!summary]
> Interview with Kevin Bai — founding FDE at Rippling, ex-Palantir, now Applied AI at Anthropic — on what forward deployed engineers actually do. FDE makes sense when a highly technical product is sold to non-technical customers, and the role wears three hats: consultant (active listening to uncover the real problem), product manager (scoping the smallest win that can be iterated), and software engineer (owning delivery end to end). Also covers interview prep — executive presence, written decision-making, code completeness — and career growth measured by expanding customer scope rather than titles.
